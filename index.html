Burp Suite helps test OWASP security risks by using tools like Proxy, Intruder, and Scanner. For Injection testing, it sends payloads to find vulnerabilities.

Authentication weaknesses are checked with brute-force attempts. Confidentiality issues are identified by intercepting and analyzing sensitive data.

Access Control is tested by manipulating sessions and parameters. Misconfiguration is flagged by the Scanner.

XSS vulnerabilities are tested by injecting scripts, and Monitoring is ensured by reviewing logs and responses for suspicious activity.
